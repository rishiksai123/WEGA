Help Section

1. How to Get Started

  • Sign Up: To access our services, start by creating an account. You can sign up as a Patient or Doctor by providing basic information like name, contact, and email.
  • Profile Setup: Complete your profile by adding necessary details like medical history (for patients) or specialization and qualifications (for doctors).
  • Login: After signing up, use your credentials to log in and access all the features of Wega.

2. How to Use the Platform

For Patients:

  • Book Appointments: Choose a doctor from the list based on specialization and availability, then book an appointment directly.
  • Video Consultations: Access your video appointments through the dashboard at the scheduled time.
  • Medicine Selection: After the consultation, view medicines prescribed by the doctor under the "Prescriptions" section.
  • Emergency Services: Use the emergency tab to request ambulance services or locate nearby hospitals.
  • Track Medical History: View and download your past consultations and prescriptions from your profile.

For Doctors:

  • Manage Appointments: View, accept, or reschedule patient bookings from your dashboard.
  • Consultations: Conduct video consultations directly through the platform.
  • Prescribe Medication: After a consultation, prescribe medicines that will be automatically shared with the patient.
  • Emergency Coordination: Guide patients during emergencies by recommending nearby hospitals or ambulance services.

3. How to Book an Appointment

  1. Step 1: Log in and go to the Appointments tab.
  2. Step 2: Select a doctor by browsing specialties and availability.
  3. Step 3: Choose a convenient time slot.
  4. Step 4: Confirm your booking and get notified via email/SMS.

4. How to Request Emergency Services

  • Ambulance Booking: Use the Emergency tab to call for an ambulance, available 24/7.
  • Nearest Hospitals: Use the Find Hospitals option to locate the nearest hospital in case of emergency.

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: How do I pay for my consultation?
    A: Payments can be made using online methods such as credit/debit cards or UPI during the booking process.
  • Q: Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?
    A: Yes, you can reschedule or cancel appointments from your dashboard at least 24 hours before the consultation.
