OUR SERVICES

Telemedicine

Telemedicine enables remote healthcare access, connecting patients with doctors through digital technology for convenient and timely care.

Medicine

Our medicine service allows doctors to prescribe medications directly on the patient’s screen, ensuring quick access to the right treatments during consultations.

Appointment

Wega's appointment booking service enables patients to schedule in-person consultations and book ambulances seamlessly within the platform at shortest time possible.

HOW TO USE OUR SERVICES?

1. Navigate to the "Services" section to explore available options like Appointment Booking and Ambulance Services.
2. Select the desired service and follow the on-screen instructions for booking.
3. Review your selections and confirm your appointment or ambulance request.
4. Track your bookings in the "My Services" section and receive notifications for updates.
