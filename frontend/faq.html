Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions

Q: How do I register on the platform?
A: You can register by clicking on the Sign Up button on the homepage and filling in the required details, such as your name, email, and contact information.
Q: How can I book an appointment with a doctor?
A: After logging in, go to the Appointments section, select a doctor based on their specialty, choose an available time slot, and confirm the booking.
Q: Can I consult with a doctor without physically visiting the clinic?
A: Yes, you can have a video consultation with doctors through our platform. Simply book an appointment and join the video call at the scheduled time.

Security and Privacy

Q: Is my medical data secure on Wega?
A: Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and follow strict data privacy protocols to ensure your medical records and consultations are secure.
Q: How do I pay for a consultation?
A: Payments can be made through various online payment methods like credit/debit cards, UPI, or digital wallets. The option will appear when confirming your booking.
Q: Can I access the platform in languages other than English?
A: Yes, our platform supports multiple languages. You can change the language settings in your profile or on the homepage.

Appointments and Emergencies

Q: What should I do in case of a medical emergency?
A: Use the Emergency Services section to book an ambulance or locate the nearest hospital immediately.
Q: Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?
A: Yes, appointments can be canceled or rescheduled from your dashboard. Please do so at least 24 hours before the scheduled time.

Prescriptions and Medical History

Q: How do doctors prescribe medicines during a consultation?
A: After the consultation, the doctor will provide a prescription that will be visible in the Prescriptions section of your profile. You can purchase the prescribed medicines online or from a local pharmacy.
Q: Can I access my previous consultations and medical history?
A: Yes, all your previous consultations and prescriptions are available in your profile under the Medical History section.
