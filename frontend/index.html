What is TELEMEDICINE?

Telemedicine refers to the use of telecommunications technology to provide healthcare services remotely, allowing patients to consult with doctors, receive diagnoses, and access treatments from their own locations. It helps reduce travel, increase convenience, and expand access to care.

Why WEGA?

Wega enhances access to healthcare, especially for people in remote or underserved areas, by enabling real-time communication between patients and healthcare providers without the need for physical visits.
